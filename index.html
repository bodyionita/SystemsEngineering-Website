Project description

Our client for the COMP204P Systems Engineering I is MegaNexus, the leading UK organization delivering data science to the government’s programme management system. Our project is named DotComDoctor, a Medical Skype, that consists of a native application that works with few hospitals in USA. Our challenge is to integrate the doctor’s side of the app and the patient’s one into one app, along with the video API.

By the end, the costumer should be able to connect to the DotComDoctor platform to register, make an appointment and initialize a video call. After that, he will meet the doctor in the Virtual Waiting Room, where they will have the discussion.
