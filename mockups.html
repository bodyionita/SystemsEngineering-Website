Mock Ups

As the bulk of the work in term 1 was towards finding out what the client required, we produced a series of wireframe mockups, as requested by our client, in order to refine the user interface of the eventual system. A series of demonstration websites were built that were base off the various features in these mockups.

After conducting research into wireframing tools, we settled on using Balsamiq to create these wireframes. The initial aim of the wireframes was to showcase the following features:

  • Login Page for GP’s
  • Patient Search
  • Patient Records Page
    • Includes lists of upcoming appointments
    • Includes list of prescriptions
  • The GP’s Schedule Page
    • Includes function to add appointments
    • View calendar in day/week/month views
    • Brief description of events in calendar view
    • Colour coded calendar entries based on type of event
  • 2-way Video/Audio consultation with patients
    • Including simultaneous ability to view patient’s records and take notes

The table below captures the iterative process we took to develop these mockups. At each stage feedback was received from the client and the mockups were then changed to reflect new requirements and suggested amendments.
